What We Do

We offer a variety of services to make your lives easier so you can spend your day doing what you want while we take care of what you need. We can deliver anything to you from any local store so long as they are not too big, too heavy and too private.

GROCERY SHOPPING

Stop wasting hours of your day traveling to the grocery store and waiting in long lines in order to get what you need. Our fast and serviceable drivers can travel to your local markets and get everything you need delivered to your door in two hours or less.

ELECTRONICS

Need a phone charger? Hairdryer break down? You are just a few clicks away; Open Gotchya and have one of our quick drivers deliver your electronic needs in a hurry.

LAUNDRY/DRYCLEANING

Stop wasting your day at the laundromat. With Gotchya we can drop off laundry and dry cleaning for you and deliver them back to your home or work as soon as they are ready.
